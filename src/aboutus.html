Scholars Pride Academy

The Scholar’s Pride Academy is a Co-Educational C.B.S.E. Curriculum School started from Nursery to Vth Standard. The SPA is housed on spacious and lush green campus on over 65000 square feet of land near Haldaur Chouraha(known as Balkishanpur Chowk) around 1 km away from at Batpura (Near 2 km mile stone) Haldaur - Ganj Road. The Scholar’s Pride Academy functions under the aegis of Charitable S.J.S.M. Shiksha Samiti Ghaziabad. The foundation stone of the Scholar’s Pride Academy was laid on 18th June 2018 by Mr. Indraveer Singh & Mr. Narendra Kumar Chaudhary in loving memory of their Parents and Grand Parents.
